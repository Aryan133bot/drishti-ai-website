DRISHTI.AI

An AI-powered cybersecurity solution designed to help individuals identify online scams and phishing messages before harm occurs.

üö® Problem Statement

Millions of people receive scam and phishing messages daily through SMS, email, and messaging applications. These messages often use fear, urgency, or false promises to manipulate users into clicking malicious links or sharing sensitive personal information.

Most individuals lack the technical knowledge to verify whether a message is genuine or fraudulent, leading to financial loss, identity theft, and emotional stress.

üí° Our Solution

DRISHTI.AI provides a simple and accessible AI-based web application where users can paste any suspicious message. The system analyzes the text and classifies it as Low, Medium, or High Risk.

Along with the risk level, DRISHTI.AI explains the reasoning in clear, non-technical language and provides general safety guidance. The goal is to support informed decision-making rather than creating fear.
